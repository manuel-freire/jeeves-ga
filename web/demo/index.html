Jeeves Demos

Jeeves is a simple Genetic Algorithm library written in JavaScript. The following demos should help you get started.

  • closest: finds the closest 2d coordinate to (.5, .5), starting from a set of random points in the square (0,0)-(2,2) and using "midpoints" as crossover function.
  • presents: finds an allocation of presents between 4 families such that everyone gives and receives 2 presents, people do not give presents to their own families (including themselves), and they never give presents twice to the same person. Other restrictions could be easily be coded in.
  • nucleo: used within an experimental moodle plugin; creates collaborative groups of students so that the total grades of groups are as balanced as possible, while taking into account pairwise "dislikes" between students (after working with a colleague, they can add it to their "do not want to work with again" list).
